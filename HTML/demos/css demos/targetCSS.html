My Fav Superheros ...

Why comic book characters? Lately, I've been doing a lot of 3D art work focused on DC's pantheon. So, of course, they're top-of-mind ðŸ˜‡ðŸ¤“ That said, my first love will always be Marvel, with characters like Hulk & Rogue as stand-out examples of an artistic choice I've been considering ...

That choice is "How should this character look?"

  • "How thick should they be?"
  • "How defined (or not)?"
  • "What parts should be more exagerated, and what should be as we'd expect?"

I've become fascinated by the challenge of communicating backstory through these physical choices. It's more than just making sure characters square with their professions etc (a self-professed professional gymnast should look more or less like other gymnasts) - physique can tell you about what people are capable of, what they care about, and even hidden character traits.

An example I've been working on lately is Superman: how do you communicate that he's different than normal humans? Make him big. But he can't be too big, or we lose connection to him as a sympathetic, human-esque character. And what about his origin story? He's a synergy: a character that should be human, except that he's an alien - so what parts of his body should be bigger? What parts smaller? It seems that for a long time, he was drawn either to look like Christopher Reeve (who had a massive chest/ribcage) or to just feel bigger - while his arms are relatively smaller (and his legs are smaller-still).
